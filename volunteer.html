Enriching Lives Through Compassionate Service

At Vatsalya Old Age Homes, every gesture of kindness helps transform loneliness into belonging. We invite you to step into the world of caregiving, where your time, talents, and presence can bring warmth to elders who once gave so much to society but now seek only love and dignity in return.

Our volunteer program is designed not just to support the daily functioning of the home, but to build lasting human connections. Whether you’re helping with basic needs, sharing stories, or simply offering company, your involvement leaves an unforgettable mark.

What You Can Look Forward To

  • One-on-one interaction and companionship with residents
  • Organizing group activities, reading sessions, and wellness routines
  • Assisting elders with digital literacy, letters, and visits
  • Participating in community awareness drives
  • Creating art, music, or therapy-based engagement sessions

Daily Engagement Flow

10:00 AM – 10:30 AM

Morning Circle

Warm introductions and mindfulness time with residents.

10:30 AM – 12:00 PM

Activity Session

Storytelling, art therapy, or games — guided by volunteers.

12:00 PM – 1:00 PM

Lunch & Companion Time

Help serve meals and share lunch with the residents.

1:00 PM – 3:00 PM

Support & Care Assistance

Walks, tech help, reading, or wellness assistance as needed.

3:00 PM – 4:00 PM

Reflection & Feedback

Group discussion with staff and other volunteers on experiences.
